journal #3

a sneak peek into my job as a phone repair assistant at Wizard Tech.

For a while now, I have worked part-time at a phone repair business, Wizard Tech, in downtown Burlington, VT. I got hired when the business was just starting out -- when I began, it was just the boss and I. While the business is primarily a place that fixes phones, we also fixes computers, tablets, hand-held devices, speakers, and much more. Throughout my time at Wizard Tech, I have had exposure to a variety of devices, tools, software, hardware, and a plethora of problems and solutions related all of them. My boss helped teach me the ins-and-outs of phone repair, and computer repair, with a concentration on mostly Apple hardware. iPhones and Macbooks have become my specialty. I also help the business by running their eBay operations. Parting out old PCs and broken phones are my primary responsibilities here, where I dissemble the device completely, then estimate its value and list the disassembled parts on eBay.
